Quick access, always on.

Vitamins make you healthy, right? But only if you follow the instructions. How many can I have, how many should my child take, with how many years will this extend my life? Is my skin improving yet?

The vitamins presented here are short bursts, small pills to swallow, performances, more instructions, inspirations. Follow the instructions correctly to open each bottle and get your fix.

In response to the quick access, always on mentality of the web, this exhibition locks its performance behind a strict set of rules to follow, including time based and only-once works. Inspired by the strict rulesets of programming and low level computer interaction, which some of us are starting to apply to our own lives.
